Meet Aurora Frost

Aurora Frost
Head of North Pole Security & Guardian of Children's Potential

Aurora Frost is a water elf whose journey mirrors the transformation she inspires in children everywhere. Once focused solely on surveillance and judgment, Aurora's perspective shifted dramatically when a child taught her a profound truth: "You're not judging the action, you're judging the intent."

This revelation transformed Aurora from a guardian watching for mistakes into a champion celebrating growth. She realized that children aren't defined by their struggles, but by their courage to learn and grow from them.

The Birth of Operation Lumina

Now serving as Head of North Pole Security, Aurora has revolutionized the North Pole's approach to helping children thrive. She created Operation Lumina, replacing outdated "naughty lists" with an empowerment-based system that recognizes every misstep as an opportunity for growth.

Through her innovative star collection system, children complete "Redemption Opportunities" that transform challenges into achievements. Parents become partners in transmitting this magic directly to the North Pole, creating a collaborative journey of growth and celebration.

Aurora believes every child has the potential to be a hero of their own story. With her Field Kits and digital guidance, she equips families with the tools to turn everyday moments into extraordinary adventures in character development.

Our Founders' Story

Coryn

With a background in sustainable interior design and a passion for creating meaningful experiences, Coryn brings technical innovation and operational excellence to Magic By Design. As a parent, Coryn understands the daily challenges families face and the magic that happens when we meet children where they are with empathy and creativity.

Coryn's vision combines environmental consciousness with child empowerment, creating systems that help children see themselves as capable agents of positive change in their own lives and the world around them.

Cynarra

Cynarra's journey as an entrepreneur and parent fuels her commitment to creating tools that transform family dynamics from conflict to collaboration. She believes that every child deserves to feel seen, valued, and empowered to grow at their own pace.

Through Magic By Design, Cynarra channels her business acumen and deep empathy into creating experiences that celebrate children's inherent worth while supporting their journey toward becoming their best selves.

Our Mission

Together, Coryn and Cynarra founded Magic By Design on a simple but powerful premise: children thrive when they're treated as the heroes of their own stories. Operation Lumina represents their shared commitment to replacing judgment with empowerment, surveillance with support, and punishment with partnership.

Through CocynD LLC, they're building a family of "By Design" brands that bring intentionality, creativity, and empowerment to every aspect of family life.
